Who are we?

Liffey Valley AC was founded in 1974 from the amalgamation of two other clubs Avondale A.C. and Inchicore A.C.

Where are we?

We are based in Islandbridge close to the Phoenix Park.

When do we train?

We train on Tuesday and Thursday nights at 7pm and Saturday mornings at 10am

Club News

Liffey Valley Womens Team Win Dublin Novice Cross Country

6 October 2013

This was the first time a Liffey Valley Womens team won a championship team award and all seven girls in action are to be congratulated on their performance. It was a very competitive team event with a record 12 teams in action.
